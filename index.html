Sustainable Fashion Brands

What is Sustainable Fashion?

Sustainable Fashion is fashion that does little to no harm to the environment.

This means the companies are making conscious efforts to reduce the impacts

they have on the environment by strategically planning to ensure they make

the least amount of impact as possible. They do things such as using

recycled materials and fabrics, plant based dyes, and disposing on wastes

in a way that does not pollute the water systems. Everlane, Patagonia,

and Siizu are a few examples of sustainable fashion brands. This companies

have done a very good job of informing the public about their clothing making process.

They pride themselves in making quality products that will last throughout the years

for their customers. Making lasting products makes it so that the customer does not

have to constantly buy clothing products and have to throw away their clothes that

will eventually end up in landfills. Clothes that end up in the landfills pollutes the environment.
